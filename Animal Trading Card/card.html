Husky

Huskies have a thick double coat that keeps them well insulated.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 21 to 23½ inches
  • Average Lifespan: 12 to 15 years
  • Habitat: Siberia

It is a medium-sized dog with a light, compact frame. Its body is well proportioned and muscular – fit for long-distance running. The male weighs between 45 and 60 pounds and the female 35 to 50 pounds.
